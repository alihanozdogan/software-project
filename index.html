Project

What do we do?

Our project is based on the idea ,to finding qualified players for your team. Let's say your team contains four players, but you need six people to create a fully-functional team , but last two players are nowhere near to found. A lot of matchcholics been in this situation with our application you will be ably to supply your team with qualified players in time and then you gonna enjoy your match.

Functionality

Our main funtionality is to filter football players in your area and invite them you can fill the missing positions in your team.

Responsive Design

The user interface is easy to use on any device. Thanks to our responsive design principles.
